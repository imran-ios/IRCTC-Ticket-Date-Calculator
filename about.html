About TKTDate Calculator

What is TKTDate Calculator?

TKTDate Calculator is a simple yet powerful tool designed to help you calculate dates by automatically adding or subtracting 60 days from your selected date.

How it works

When you first load the page, the calculator automatically adds 60 days to today's date. When you select a different date, it subtracts 60 days from your selection and displays the result.
