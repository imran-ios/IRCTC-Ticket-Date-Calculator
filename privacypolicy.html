Privacy Policy

Information Collection

TKTDate Calculator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All calculations are performed locally in your browser.

Data Storage

No data is stored on our servers. The dates you select and calculations performed remain private and are not saved anywhere. Your theme preference is saved locally in your browser.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies. Theme preferences are stored using local browser storage only.
