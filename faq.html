Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the calculator?

Simply click on the date picker and select a date from the calendar. The calculator will automatically subtract 60 days and show you the result.

How do I switch between light and dark themes?

Click the theme toggle button (moon/sun icon) in the top right corner to switch between light and dark modes.

What happens when I first load the page?

The calculator automatically adds 60 days to today's date and displays it as the initial result.

Can I calculate different day intervals?

Currently, the calculator is set to work with 60-day intervals. Future versions may include customizable intervals.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, TKTDate Calculator is completely free to use with no registration required.

Does it work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! The calculator is designed to work seamlessly on all devices including smartphones and tablets.
